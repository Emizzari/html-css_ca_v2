ABOUT US

Who are we?

We are a group of local bikers, who enjoy exploring and experience the nature around us. We also care about the nature and ocean on our doorstep and want to preserve our enviorment.

Why is it important?

Clean environment is essential for healthy living. The more you don’t care about our environment, the more it will become polluted with contaminants and toxins that have a harmful impact on our health. Air pollution can cause respiratory diseases and cancer, among other problems and diseases. Water pollution can lead to typhoid and diarrheal diseases.

How we do it

We do it by taking in and fixing old bicycles, donated to us by the locals in Risør. This is how we came up with the name "Re-Cycle", as we recycled old bicycles. The tracks are then replaced with organic material and 10% of the profit goes to the charity "Ocean Conservancy"

Ocean Conservancy

Ocean Conservancy is working to protect the ocean from today's global challenges. They create science-based solutions for a healty ocean and wildlife. We have choosen this charity as the ocean is close to our hearts in Risør, and we want to keep it as healty as possible

"Our work is focused on solving some of the greatest threats facing our ocean today. From the Arctic to the Gulf of Mexico, we bring people, science and policy together, to champion innovative solutions and fight for a sustainable ocean."

Things you can do for the enviorment

  • Use reusable bags
  • Print as little as necessary
  • Recycle
  • Use a reusable beverage containers
  • Use energy-efficient light bulbs instead of regular bulbs
  • Avoid taking cars or carpool when possible
