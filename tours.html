TOURS

City tour

  • Prices: 125kr
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Time: 1 Hour

Risør has a long and proud tradition of building wooden boats and houses. We will take you through the history of the streets in Risør and teach you about the old history and secret behind why all the houses are all white, and about a devestating fire 200 years ago

ORDER NOW

Krana

  • Prices: 125kr
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Time: 1.5 Hour

Krana is a roundtour which follows the coastal road on the outher side of Risør city centre. We teach more about the ship building process in our litle town. If we are lucky, they might be working on building a boat while we pass by. You can often spot seals from this road

ORDER NOW

Urheia

  • Prices: 150kr
  • Difficulty: Medium
  • Time: 2 Hour

Urheia is an recreational area and cultural trial close to Risør City centre. The germans under the second world war built a costoal fortress in this area. Many buildings are still standing and it is possible to take a look inside. The lakes in Urheia is also perfect for swimming

ORDER NOW

Fransåsen

  • Prices: 175kr
  • Difficulty: Hard
  • Time: 3 Hour

Fransåsen is a 12 kilometer long hike that starts from Frydendal Vest. From there we will follow a blue marked trail through uneven terrain. There are fantastic views from the top at Fransåsen. This tour is the longest and hardest tours that we offer, but it also the most rewarding

ORDER NOW
